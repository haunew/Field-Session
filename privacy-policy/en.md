# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 24, 2026**

Welcome to Field Session. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Field Session mobile application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Username or nickname you set up in the App.
- **Session Content**: Text notes, expense records, and other information you manually input during a field session.
- **Photos**: Images you capture through the App's camera feature during a field session.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Location Data**: When you start a field session, the App collects your geographic location at a low frequency to build a timeline. Location collection stops immediately when you end the session.
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, and App version for compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.
- **Usage Data**: Session start/end times, feature usage frequency, and crash logs to help us improve the App.

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect location data outside of active field sessions.
- We do **not** access your contacts, call logs, messages, or any data unrelated to the App's functionality.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide core functionality: session tracking, timeline generation, note-taking, photo capture, and expense logging.
- Generate personal reports that you can export.
- Improve App performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience.
- Ensure the security and integrity of the App.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- All session data (notes, photos, location records) is stored **locally on your device** by default.
- Exported reports are generated locally and shared only at your discretion.
- We employ industry-standard security measures including encryption to protect any data transmitted or stored.
- We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. Data Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **With your consent**: When you explicitly choose to export or share reports.
- **Legal requirements**: When required by applicable laws, regulations, or legal proceedings.
- **Service providers**: With trusted partners who assist in App operation (e.g., crash analytics), bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data stored in the App.
- **Delete** your session data, notes, photos, and records at any time.
- **Export** your data in standard formats.
- **Withdraw consent** for location access through your device's system settings.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 14 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or other appropriate means. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
